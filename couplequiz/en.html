CoupleQuiz Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026 · Applies to CoupleQuiz for iOS

TL;DR: CoupleQuiz does not collect your personal information. Everything stays on your device, except purchase receipts which are processed by Apple and RevenueCat for subscription management.

1. What we don't collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, government ID)
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • No usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports
  • No advertising SDKs and no analytics SDKs

2. Where your data lives

Everything you create inside CoupleQuiz — unlocked packs, language preference, game progress — is stored locally in the app's iOS sandbox. It never leaves your device through our servers.

Uninstalling the app deletes all local data. Your subscription and one-time purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID via the "Restore Purchases" feature.

3. Third-party services

CoupleQuiz uses the following third-party services strictly to enable in-app purchases (IAP):

  • Apple StoreKit: Apple's official in-app purchase framework, used to process one-time pack unlocks (Deep Talk, Spicy, Therapy, etc.) and subscriptions. Purchase receipts are sent to Apple's servers for validation. Data handling follows the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: A third-party subscription management platform that helps us manage subscription state and handle "Restore Purchases". RevenueCat receives anonymous purchase state queries keyed by Apple-generated anonymous identifiers; no name, email, or personal identifiers are sent. Data handling follows the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Beyond these two services, CoupleQuiz does not send data to any other third party.

4. Network usage

CoupleQuiz only uses the network in these situations:

  • When you start a purchase or subscribe — Apple StoreKit communicates with Apple's servers.
  • When you tap "Restore Purchases" — your subscription state is queried.

Regular usage — flipping cards, answering prompts, switching packs — works fully offline once the pack is unlocked.

5. Permissions

CoupleQuiz does not request sensitive permissions such as contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera. The Info.plist declares NSUserTrackingUsageDescription as a standard iOS field, but the current version of CoupleQuiz does not perform any cross-app tracking and the tracking authorization prompt is not triggered.

6. Children's privacy

CoupleQuiz is a conversation game designed for adult couples and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If a future release changes how data is handled (for example, integrating a new third-party service), we will notify users in-app and update the "Last updated" date on this page.
