CoupleQuiz Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026 · Applies to CoupleQuiz for iOS

TL;DR: CoupleQuiz is a one-time paid download. It does not collect your personal information, contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions, and works fully offline after install. Everything stays on your device.

1. What we don't collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, government ID)
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • No usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports
  • No advertising SDKs and no analytics SDKs

2. Where your data lives

Everything you create inside CoupleQuiz — language preference, game progress, saved cards — is stored locally in the app's iOS sandbox. It never leaves your device through our servers.

Uninstalling the app deletes all local data. Your record of having purchased the app is kept in your Apple account; you can re-download CoupleQuiz for free on any iOS device signed in with the same Apple ID.

3. Third-party services

CoupleQuiz is a one-time paid download. It contains no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads, and therefore integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, subscription management, or tracking SDKs.

The download and payment flow itself is handled by the Apple App Store and Apple StoreKit, governed by the Apple Privacy Policy. As the developer, we do not see your payment details, name, email, or any other data from your Apple account.

4. Network usage

After install, CoupleQuiz runs fully offline. All question cards are bundled inside the app — nothing needs to be downloaded or synced while you use it.

5. Permissions

CoupleQuiz does not request sensitive permissions such as contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera. The Info.plist declares NSUserTrackingUsageDescription as a standard iOS field, but the current version of CoupleQuiz does not perform any cross-app tracking and the tracking authorization prompt is not triggered.

6. Children's privacy

CoupleQuiz is a conversation game designed for adult couples and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If a future release changes how data is handled (for example, integrating a new third-party service), we will notify users in-app and update the "Last updated" date on this page.
