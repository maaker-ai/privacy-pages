常见问题

如何恢复已购买的 Pack？

如果你换了新手机、重装了 App，或者在另一台 iOS 设备上登录，已购内容可以免费恢复：打开 App 进入 设置，点击 恢复购买（Restore Purchases），确保登录的 Apple ID 与当初购买时一致即可。

如何取消订阅？

订阅管理由 Apple 统一处理，App 内无法直接取消。请进入 iOS「设置」 > 点击顶部你的 Apple ID > 订阅，找到 CoupleQuiz，点击「取消订阅」即可。取消后，当前订阅周期结束前你仍可继续使用付费内容。

这款 App 会收集我的数据吗？

不会。CoupleQuiz 不需要注册账号，不收集你的姓名、邮箱、位置、通讯录等任何个人信息。所有本地数据（解锁状态、语言偏好、进度）都保存在你的设备上。唯一经过网络的是 Apple 和 RevenueCat 处理订阅所必需的购买凭据。详见 隐私政策。

购买后没有立即解锁怎么办？

请先确认设备网络正常，然后在 App 内点击「恢复购买」。如仍未解锁，请联系我们并附上 App Store 购买收据邮件截图（包含订单号），我们会尽快协助处理。

CoupleQuiz Support

Last updated: April 20, 2026

CoupleQuiz is an iOS conversation card game for couples. 150+ carefully crafted question cards to help you and your partner reconnect, explore new sides of each other, and spark deeper conversations. Below are the most common questions and how to reach us.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've switched phones, reinstalled the app, or signed in on another iOS device, your previous purchases can be restored for free: open the app, go to Settings, tap Restore Purchases, and make sure the Apple ID signed in is the same one used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscription management is handled by Apple; it cannot be cancelled from inside the app. Open iOS Settings > tap your Apple ID at the top > Subscriptions, find CoupleQuiz, and tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll retain access to paid content until the end of the current billing period.

Does this app collect my data?

No. CoupleQuiz does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any personal information. All local data (unlocked packs, language preference, progress) stays on your device. The only network traffic is the purchase receipt flow required by Apple and RevenueCat for subscription management. See the Privacy Policy for details.

My purchase didn't unlock immediately — what should I do?

First, check that you have a working internet connection, then tap "Restore Purchases" in the app. If it's still not unlocked, email us with a screenshot of the App Store purchase receipt (including the order ID) and we'll sort it out for you.
