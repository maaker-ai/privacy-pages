常见问题

如何在新设备上使用？

CoupleQuiz 绑定你购买时使用的 Apple ID。在新设备上，只要用同一个 Apple ID 登录 App Store，打开 App Store 进入 账户 > 已购项目，找到 CoupleQuiz 即可免费重新下载；也可以直接在 App Store 搜索「CoupleQuiz」，下载按钮会显示为免费。

如何退款？

本 App 的退款由 Apple 统一处理。请访问 reportaproblem.apple.com，用你购买时的 Apple ID 登录，找到 CoupleQuiz 的订单申请退款。我们作为开发者无法直接处理 Apple 收取的款项退款。

这款 App 会收集我的数据吗？

不会。CoupleQuiz 不需要注册账号，不收集你的姓名、邮箱、位置、通讯录等任何个人信息，也不包含任何分析或广告 SDK。所有本地数据（语言偏好、游戏进度）都保存在你的设备上。下载与付款由 Apple App Store 处理。详见 隐私政策。

付款后 App 没有下载下来怎么办？

请先确认设备网络正常，重启一下 App Store。如仍未下载，可打开 App Store 进入 账户 > 已购项目 手动重新下载。如果问题依然存在，请联系我们并附上 App Store 购买收据邮件截图（包含订单号），我们会尽快协助处理。

Frequently asked questions

How do I use the app on a new device?

CoupleQuiz is tied to the Apple ID you used to purchase it. On a new device, sign in to the App Store with that same Apple ID, open the App Store, go to Account > Purchased, find CoupleQuiz, and tap the download icon — it will re-download for free. You can also search for "CoupleQuiz" directly in the App Store; the download button will show as free.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for this app are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with the Apple ID used for the purchase, locate the CoupleQuiz order, and submit a refund request. As the developer, we cannot directly refund payments collected by Apple.

Does this app collect my data?

No. CoupleQuiz does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any personal information. It contains no analytics or advertising SDKs. All local data (language preference, game progress) stays on your device. The download and payment flow is handled by the Apple App Store. See the Privacy Policy for details.

I paid but the app didn't download — what should I do?

Check that you have a working internet connection and restart the App Store. If the download still doesn't start, open the App Store, go to Account > Purchased, and manually tap re-download. If the issue persists, email us with a screenshot of the App Store purchase receipt (including the order ID) and we'll sort it out.
