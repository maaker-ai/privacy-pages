DementiaLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026 · Applies to DementiaLog iOS

TL;DR
  • We collect health observations you record, voice/photo attachments, and account info. We do NOT sell your data.
  • AI processing (transcription, classification, summary) happens on our servers. Audio is deleted after transcription unless you opt to keep it.
  • You can export or delete all data anytime. Family members you invite see only the timeline you share.
  • We are not a HIPAA Business Associate by default. If you need a BAA, contact us — paid Family/Enterprise plans (coming soon).

1. What We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (from Apple Sign-in or Google Sign-in)
  • Display name (you provide)
  • Apple/Google subject ID (for authentication)

Patient Information You Provide

  • Patient name (or pseudonym you choose)
  • Birth year (not full birthdate by default)
  • Dementia stage (MCI / Early / Middle / Late, optional)
  • Relationship to you
  • ZIP code (optional, for local Benefits lookup)

Care Observations You Record

  • Voice recordings (processed and deleted within 24 hours unless you toggle "Keep audio")
  • Transcribed text from voice
  • Photos you attach to events
  • Tags AI assigns (Behavior / Medication / Mood / etc.)
  • Timestamps and optional location
  • Comments and reactions from family members
  • Medication entries and adherence logs
  • Caregiver burnout self-assessments (private to you, never shared)
  • Apple HealthKit data (only if you grant permission, only for the patient you choose)

Technical Data

  • Device type, OS version, App version (for crash reports)
  • IP address (transient, for security/rate-limiting; not stored long-term)
  • Crash logs (anonymized)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real-time location (only ZIP code if you provide it)
  • Contacts list
  • Photos outside what you explicitly attach
  • Microphone access outside active recording sessions
  • Any health data from Apple Health unless you explicitly grant access

You can:

  • Export: Settings → Data → Export All Data (JSON file emailed to you)
  • Delete account: Settings → Account → Delete Account (irreversible after 7-day grace period)

5. Data Sharing & Family Members

  • Family members you invite via DementiaLog Family Invite have access to the specific patient timeline you share, with the role you assign (Spouse / Child / Sibling / Professional / Friend).
  • Caregiver burnout self-assessments and your private notes are never shared with anyone.
  • You can revoke any caregiver's access anytime in Settings → Family.
  • Removing a caregiver does not delete past entries they made; it only revokes future access.

6. Security

  • All data in transit uses TLS 1.3
  • Database at rest is encrypted (AES-256)
  • Sensitive medical fields (notes, transcriptions) are encrypted with per-user keys derived from your auth
  • Voice recordings, before transcription, are stored in encrypted object storage with short-lived access URLs
  • We follow OWASP best practices and conduct internal security reviews quarterly
  • We do not publicly claim HIPAA compliance, but our architecture follows HIPAA Technical Safeguards principles

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / Other)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you (free, within 30 days)
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate data (use in-app editing)
  • Erase: Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Account)
  • Restrict: Pause AI processing (Settings → AI → Pause AI)
  • Port: Export to JSON
  • Object: Opt out of analytics (Settings → Privacy → Disable Analytics)
  • Complain: Contact your local data protection authority

For California residents (CCPA): we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

For EU/UK residents (GDPR): our Data Controller is Maaker.AI (contact below). We do not transfer EU data to non-adequate jurisdictions without appropriate safeguards.

8. Children's Privacy

DementiaLog is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are under 18, do not use this service. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us for immediate deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes (new data uses, new third parties) will be notified via:

  • In-app notification 7 days before taking effect
  • Email to your account email

Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

Important Disclaimers

DementiaLog is a personal observation log and family communication tool. It is:

  • NOT a medical device
  • NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • NOT designed to detect, diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease

AI summaries and behavior predictions are based on patterns in the data you record. They are not clinical assessments. Always consult qualified healthcare providers for medical decisions.

In case of emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in US, 999 in UK, 120 in China).
