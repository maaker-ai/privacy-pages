Memento Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026 · Applies to Memento for HarmonyOS

Short version: Memento collects zero data. No sign-up, no accounts, nothing leaves your device.

1. What we don't collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, ID numbers)
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA, OAID)
  • No usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports

2. Where your data lives

Everything you save inside Memento—birthday, life expectancy, custom milestones, language preference, saved quotes—stays in the app's private directory on your device. Nothing is sent over the network.

Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

3. No third-party SDKs

Memento does not integrate any analytics, advertising, push, or tracking SDK. Font assets are bundled with the app and are not downloaded at runtime.

4. Permissions

Memento requests only one permission:

  • ohos.permission.INTERNET: reserved for potential future update checks. The current version does not use the network.

5. Children

Memento is not directed at children under 14 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Changes to this policy

If a future version of Memento changes how we handle data (for example, introduces cloud sync), we will notify users in-app and update this page.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach out:
