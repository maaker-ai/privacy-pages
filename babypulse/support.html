常见问题

如何在新设备上恢复数据？

BabyPulse 的本地数据（喂养记录、睡眠记录、宝宝档案）保存在当前设备，不会自动跨设备同步。如果换新 iPhone，建议先在旧设备上使用「导出」功能（若已开启）或手动记录关键数据，再卸载 App。目前没有云端备份功能。

如何恢复已购买的 Pro 解锁？

Pro 功能是一次性内购（终身有效），绑定你购买时使用的 Apple ID。在新设备上用同一 Apple ID 登录 App Store，进入 App 的付费页点击「Restore Purchases」即可恢复。

如何申请退款？

内购退款由 Apple 统一处理。请访问 reportaproblem.apple.com，用购买时的 Apple ID 登录，找到 BabyPulse 的订单申请退款。作为开发者我们无法直接处理 Apple 收取的款项。

这款 App 会收集我的数据吗？

不会。BabyPulse 不需要注册账号，不收集你或宝宝的个人身份信息。所有记录都保存在设备本地，不经过网络。详见 隐私政策。

BabyPulse Support

Last updated: April 24, 2026

BabyPulse is an iOS baby tracker designed for new parents to log feeds, sleep, and diaper changes. Supports multiple baby profiles and local history. Below are the most common questions and how to reach us.

Frequently asked questions

How do I transfer data to a new device?

BabyPulse stores local data (feed logs, sleep logs, baby profiles) on the current device and does not automatically sync across devices. If you're switching iPhones, consider using the export feature (if available) or noting key data before uninstalling. No cloud backup is available yet.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Pro is a one-time in-app purchase (lifetime) tied to your Apple ID. On the new device, sign in with the same Apple ID in App Store, open the BabyPulse paywall, and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore access.

How do I request a refund?

IAP refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with the Apple ID used for the purchase, locate the BabyPulse order, and request a refund. As the developer we cannot directly refund Apple-collected payments.

Does this app collect my data?

No. BabyPulse does not require an account and does not collect personal information about you or your baby. All records stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Found a bug or have a feature request?

Email us with a description + device model + iOS version + optional screenshots. We'll get back to you promptly.
